What We Offer

All work is carried out to a high standard by our professional team of highly skilled painters. From building to baseing we offer all services you will need for every step of your project, choose from 3 different paint levels to suit your needs, all paints, washes and glues are all Games Workshop official products so you can rest assured we only use top quality materials. You can track the progress of your order from start to finish via your accounts page.



Assembly

Need your models build and primed before painting? Let us do the work for you at a low cost. All work is carried out to a high standard.


Painting

Choose your required level of painting, Basic, Table Top or Display. Look at our gallery for details of what is included in each level of painting.



Basing

Basing is the finishing touch your models need, it adds realism and depth to you models, choose from different basing looks and textures.


Delivery

Once your project is fully complete we aim to get it back to you ASAP. We use tracked next day delivery to ensure you recieve your models safely and in good order.
